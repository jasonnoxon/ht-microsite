Build Communities

Healthy Together is a referral network that powers a community-wide care network. We've named our network Healthy Together to call to our desire to have this tool serve and be owned by our entire community.

Healthy Together allows those participating in the network throughout Albany, Fulton, Montgomery, Rensselaer, Saratoga, and Schenectady counties to connect in real time with social service providers such as housing agencies, behavioral health providers, and food pantries.
